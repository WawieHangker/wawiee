Tips have a healthy lifestyle

Balance Diet

Eating in moderation helps prevent overeating.

Drink plenty of water throughout the day.

Regular Physical Activity

Include strength training exercises at least twice a week to build muscle and bone strength.

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity each week (e.g., brisk walking, cycling).

Avoid Harmful Habits

Stay socially connected with friends and family.

Set aside time for hobbies and activities that bring joy.
