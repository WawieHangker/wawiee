What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple measure used to classify individuals based on their body weight relative to their height. It helps indicate whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. Despite its widespread use, BMI doesn't differentiate between muscle and fat, so it may not always accurately reflect an individual's health or fitness.
